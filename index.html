About Us



Operating since 1996, M&J Garage is one of the leading Car Hire companies in Gozo specialising in Car Rentals and Taxi Service.

Our offices are situated in the picturesque village of Nadur, which its evergreen valleys lead you to three of the most beautiful beaches on the island - the beach of Dahlet Qorrot and the red sandy beaches of San Blas bay and Ramla bay.

Our objectives are to give our esteemed clientele a reliable 24-hour service and this can be achieved by our friendly staff and also by our fleet of cars, which are kept in a very good condition. This will make a memorable and unforgettable holiday to our customers.

Transfers

We offer a 24/7 chauffeur-driven transfer service around Gozo and airport transfers upon request. Booking is recommended for large groups.

Airport Transfers

We can provide Split or Direct transfers to and from the airport and your accommodation in Gozo.
A Direct transfer includes a personal driver from the Airport to the accommodation in Gozo (or from Gozo to the Airport). A split transfer consists of two trips, one between the ferry and the airport and another in Gozo.

Gozo Transfers

Our transfer service in Gozo is available 24/7 from any location.

Day Tours

Day tours around Malta and Gozo are available upon request.
Tour hours are from 09:00 to 16:00 in Malta and 10:00 to 16:00 in Gozo. The standard tours visit the main places of interest but can be customised upon request.
