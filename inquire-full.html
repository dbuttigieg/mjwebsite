Rental Terms

Included in the Price

  • Delivery and pick-up of the vehicle from any location around Gozo
  • 24/7 Roadside assistance
  • Road licensing fee
  • Local taxes
  • Unlimited kilometres

Not Included in the Price

  • Delivery charges for locations in Malta
  • Local charges such as speeding tickets which could be incurred during the rental period.
  • Other fees for extras such as baby seats, collision damage waiver and additional driver (unless quoted in the price upon inquiry).

Fuel Policy

  • The vehicle will be delivered with an amount of fuel, and the hirer must return the vehicle with the same amount of fuel. Failure to do so shall result in extra charges.
  • Vehicle fuel shall be for hirer's account

Diving Equipment

  • Diving equipment may only be carried in Jeeps.

General

  • The vehicle will be delivered clean, with all tyres, tools, accessories and equipment in good condition. Everything shall be returned in the same condition.
  • Cars are rented on a 24-hour basis.
  • Drivers must be over 21 years of age to drive Family, Full-size, and 7-seater cars and over 25 years to drive Jeeps, in possession of a valid driving license for at least 2 years.
  • Vehicle is covered by a fully-comprehensive insurance policy. The hirer may pay a collision damage waiver fee to reduce the excess fee.
